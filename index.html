About the reader

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the Scripture text?

Simple Liturgy is meant to be paired with a physical Bible. It provides the appointed citations and prayers; open your Bible to read each passage.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Load or install the app once while online so the reader, prayers, and lectionary can be saved. It will then work in airplane mode, though reconnecting occasionally allows updates and restores data if your browser clears stored files.

What is Beginner's Apprenticeship?

Beginner's Apprenticeship creates inter-denominational books, music, podcasts, and simple liturgies that adapt historic Christian prayer rhythms for families and households with small children.
